What is White Rice?

White Rice is a carbohydrate / energy source used in dog food. On a label it may also appear as: white rice, rice.

What to look for on the label

Ingredients are listed by weight, so the ones near the top make up most of the recipe. White Rice is worth noting wherever it appears.
