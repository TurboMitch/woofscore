What is Wheat Gluten?

Wheat Gluten is a low-cost filler used in dog food. On a label it may also appear as: wheat gluten.

What to look for on the label

Ingredients are listed by weight, so the ones near the top make up most of the recipe. Wheat Gluten is best seen low on the list — or not at all. The fastest way to judge a whole product is to paste its full ingredient list into our free dog food checker, which grades it A–F and flags every ingredient like this one.

How Wheat Gluten affects the WoofScore

Our grade rewards named whole proteins, vegetables, fruit and beneficial supplements, and penalises vague by-products, cheap fillers, artificial colours and controversial preservatives.
