What is Animal By-Products?

Animal By-Products is a protein source used in dog food. On a label it may also appear as: animal by-products, by-products, by products.

What to look for on the label

Ingredients are listed by weight, so the ones near the top make up most of the recipe. Animal By-Products is best seen low on the list — or not at all. The fastest way to judge a whole product is to paste its full ingredient list into our free dog food checker, which grades it A–F and flags every ingredient like this one.

How Animal By-Products affects the WoofScore

Our grade rewards named whole proteins, vegetables, fruit and beneficial supplements, and penalises vague by-products, cheap fillers, artificial colours and controversial preservatives. Animal By-Products contributes as a watch-tier item; read the full scoring methodology to see exactly how the grade is calculated.
