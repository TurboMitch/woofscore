Veterinary review

Dog nutrition is a health topic, so we hold our content to a higher bar.

WoofScore's scoring criteria and our "can dogs eat…" guidance are built on AAFCO ingredient definitions, FDA pet-food guidance and established veterinary-nutrition references, and our toxic-food classifications follow widely accepted veterinary and pet-poison-control sources.

We are establishing ongoing review by a licensed veterinarian, and this page will name that reviewer, with their credentials, once in place. Until then, please treat all content as educational rather than clinical, and consult your own veterinarian about your dog's diet.

Are you a licensed vet or veterinary nutritionist interested in reviewing our criteria? We'd love to hear from you: hello@woofscore.com.

Informational only, not veterinary advice. In an emergency, contact your vet or a pet poison helpline.
