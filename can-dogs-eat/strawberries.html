Why strawberries is fine for dogs

Strawberries are safe in moderation — they're full of fibre and vitamin C. Serve fresh, washed and cut, not in syrup.

How much can a dog eat?

Treats — including strawberries — should make up no more than about 10% of your dog's daily calories. Start with a small amount the first time to make sure it agrees with your dog, and serve it plain, with no added salt, sugar, oil or seasoning.

A few caveats

Puppies, seniors and small breeds need smaller portions. Dogs with health conditions (diabetes, kidney disease, a sensitive stomach) should only have new foods with your vet's okay.
