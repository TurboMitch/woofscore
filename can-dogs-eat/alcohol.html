Can dogs eat alcohol?

🍺 Toxic — never feed

Even small amounts of alcohol (drinks, raw dough) can cause dangerous drops in blood sugar, temperature and breathing.

If eaten: Seek veterinary care immediately.

The bottom line

Do not feed alcohol to your dog — it is toxic and even small amounts can be dangerous. When in doubt, call your vet or a pet poison helpline.
