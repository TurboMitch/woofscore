Can dogs eat yogurt?

🥛 Okay with care

Plain, unsweetened yogurt in small amounts can be fine and provides probiotics — avoid flavoured/sweetened (xylitol).

Built on AAFCO & FDA pet-food criteria · Last updated June 2026 · How we rate

How to give yogurt safely

Plain, unsweetened yogurt in small amounts can be fine and provides probiotics — avoid flavoured/sweetened (xylitol).

How much and how to prepare

Keep it to small, occasional amounts, served plain. Remove any pits, seeds, cores, skins, bones, shells or added seasoning first, and introduce it gradually.

Who should avoid it

Puppies, seniors, small breeds and dogs with health conditions should have only tiny amounts, or skip it. When unsure, check with your vet.
