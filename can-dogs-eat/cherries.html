Can dogs eat cherries?

🍒 Toxic — never feed

The pits, stems and leaves contain cyanide and the pits can cause blockages. The flesh alone isn't toxic but cherries are best avoided.

If eaten: If your dog ate pits, watch for choking or blockage signs.

The bottom line

Do not feed cherries to your dog — it is toxic and even small amounts can be dangerous. When in doubt, call your vet or a pet poison helpline.
