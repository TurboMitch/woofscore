Can dogs eat carrots?

🥕 Safe in moderation

Carrots are a low-calorie, crunchy favourite — good for teeth and rich in beta-carotene. Raw or cooked.

The bottom line

​Carrots is generally safe for dogs in small amounts as an occasional treat, prepared plainly with no added salt, sugar, oil or seasoning. Treats should make up no more than about 10% of your dog’s daily calories.
