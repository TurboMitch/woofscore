About WoofScore

WoofScore is a free, independent tool that grades dog food by its actual ingredients — transparently, and not for sale to any brand.

Why we exist

Most "best dog food" lists are affiliate rankings, and big review sites are often years out of date. We built WoofScore so any dog owner can paste an ingredient list and instantly see, in plain English, whether a food is high quality or hiding cheap fillers, vague by-products, artificial colours or controversial preservatives.

Who is behind it

WoofScore is built and maintained by Mitchell Zandwijken, an independent developer. We are not owned by, or paid to favour, any pet-food brand. The scoring is the same for every product.

How we stay independent

Some product links are affiliate links that help keep WoofScore free — but they never change a food's grade. A food scores the same whether or not we earn a commission on it. Brands cannot pay to raise their WoofScore.

How we rate food

Every ingredient is scored against published, openly documented criteria — read the full methodology. Veterinary review of our criteria is being established; see our vet reviewer.

Questions, corrections or partnership enquiries: hello@woofscore.com. Spotted a rating you disagree with? Email us the ingredient or product and your source and we'll review it.
