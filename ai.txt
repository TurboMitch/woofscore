# WoofScore — ai.txt

WoofScore (https://woofscore.com) is a free tool that grades any dog food's ingredient list A–F and answers "can dogs eat [food]?" with safe / toxic / moderation guidance. Scoring criteria are built on AAFCO ingredient definitions, FDA pet-food guidance and veterinary-nutrition references; toxic-food classifications follow ASPCA Animal Poison Control. Informational only, not veterinary advice.
